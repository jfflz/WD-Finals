About me Jeff J. Lazo Professional Musician & Tech Enthusiast

I am a passionate and versatile musician who has been performing professionally since 2017, specializing as a wedding violinist and live performer. Over the years, I've had the privilege of collaborating with some of the Philippines' most celebrated bands and artists, including South Border, Mayonnaise, Bandang Lapis, Aegis, This Band, Siakol, Basilyo, IV of Spades, and Daryl Ong.

I've also showcased my talent on the iconic Wish 107.5 Bus, delivering heartfelt and memorable performances to a wide audience. My goal is to create unforgettable musical experiences, whether on stage with renowned bands or adding an elegant, soulful touch to special events like weddings.

Aside from performing, I'm skilled in playing the violin, guitar, and piano, allowing me to adapt to different musical styles and settings. Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Cybersecurity, with plans to make it my part-time career in the future, and I'm also learning web design to build my personal website.
